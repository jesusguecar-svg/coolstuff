1) **Plain-English definition (exam-tested fact)**

### юИАentityюИВ["organization","Medicaid","joint federal-state health coverage program"]юИБ

1) **Plain-English definition (exam-tested fact)**  
Medicaid is a **joint federalтАУstate program** that provides health coverage to certain **low-income** individuals and specific eligibility groups (children, pregnant women, certain parents, seniors, and people with disabilities), administered by states under federal requirements and jointly funded by federal and state dollars. юИАciteюИВturn15view2юИВturn15view1юИВturn15view0юИБ

### WorkersтАЩ compensation

1) **Plain-English definition (exam-tested in concept; Texas-specific details commonly tested)**

### Medicaid
